Project Overview:

The Graphics Design Collage is a curated visual portfolio created by Sumana to showcase diverse design skills and creative versatility. The collage integrates multiple works—including logos, posters, advertisements, book covers, thumbnails, certificates, and social media designs—into a single cohesive composition. It reflects both artistic vision and technical proficiency, allowing potential clients and collaborators to see the range of services in a visually engaging snapshot.

Serving as both a portfolio pieceand a marketing tool, the collage represents a blend of experimentation, professional design principles, and humor-infused creativity.

‍

Objectives:

  • Visual Showcase– Provide a single, consolidated representation of various graphic design works.
  • Demonstrate Range– Highlight different styles, mediums, and formats of design (digital, print, branding, promotional).
  • Attract Clients– Present creative capabilities to businesses, individuals, and institutions seeking design services.
  • Portfolio Strengthening– Enhance the Hephaestus website and personal brand with visual proof of expertise.
  • Creative Expression– Illustrate not just technical ability but also a unique artistic voice and humor-driven style.

    • ‍

    Scope of Work:

    Design Elements

    • Logo Designs– Branding identities for businesses and individuals.
    • Posters & Advertisements– Promotional visuals for events, campaigns, and marketing.
    • Book Covers & Certificates– Print-ready creative works blending typography and visuals.
    • Thumbnails & Social Media Posts– Digital assets optimized for online platforms.
    • Miscellaneous Creative Assets– Conceptual designs showcasing humor, experimentation, and stylistic diversity.

    Collage Composition

    • Selection of best works from various categories.
    • Structured arrangement for visual balance and storytelling.
    • Color harmonization to reflect vibrancy and diversity of projects.
    • Exported in high-resolution for digital and print display.

    Integration & Presentation

    • Featured on the Hephaestus website under services/portfolio.
    • Can be repurposed for presentations, proposals, and social media promotion.
    • Acts as a “visual elevator pitch” to capture client interest instantly.

    Future Enhancements

    • Expand collage with new projects over time.
    • Create themed collages (e.g., branding-only, social media-only).
    • Develop an interactive online version where users can click on a design to view details.
